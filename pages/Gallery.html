    Our interior

    while the Italian restaurants can vary in their interior, from classic to modern, we are proud to be stand out in our designs as authentically Italian! Partly that is the reason that all 3 of our dining halls are dedicated to representing three significant regions of Italian culture. The Northern Italian hall features a neoclassic, Milenesque interior, while the Southern Italian hall is full of colors, hues and stands for a Napoli style… The Sicilian hall we have is the most vibrant, designed in a red and blue style, just like a Vesuvius surrounded by the blue sea…
